Dr. Omar Mohammad Abdullah Al-Ansari

Dr. Omar Mohammad Abdullah Al-Ansari

Member

Dr. Omar Al Ansari is President of Qatar University, and an academic leader with a distinguished career at Qatar University. After obtaining his PhD in Civil Engineering from Texas University in 1999, he began his journey at Qatar University as a faculty member in the Civil Engineering Department.

Throughout his tenure, Dr. Al Ansari demonstrated exceptional leadership skills and was entrusted with various administrative roles. In 2003, he was appointed as the Dean of Student Affairs, assuming responsibility for shaping the student experience and fostering a supportive campus environment. Building on his success, he was promoted to the position of Assistant to the Vice President for Student Affairs in 2004, further expanding his impact on student-related initiatives.

Dr. Al Ansari's served as the Vice President for Student Affairs from 2007 to 2015. During his tenure, he spearheaded transformative advancements in digital platform systems that managed student information. His approach and dedication to innovation led to the development of a robust information system for employees, positively influencing the overall efficiency and effectiveness of the university's administrative processes.

In 2017, Dr. Omar Al Ansari was appointed Deputy to the University President for Academic Affairs, where he played a pivotal role in implementing university projects, including the development and execution of Qatar University's comprehensive academic plan. With a keen eye for organizational excellence, he reviewed and refined the academic sector's structure, culminating in the establishment of a deanship for general studies. Additionally, he created key positions such as Assistant to the Vice President for Academic Planning and Quality Assurance and Assistant to the Vice President for Faculty Affairs, further bolstering the university's commitment to academic excellence.

Driven by his commitment to enhance teaching and learning practices, Dr. Al Ansari led the development process of the Center for Excellence in Teaching & Learning. His visionary leadership ensured that teaching methods aligned seamlessly with Qatar University's mission and strategy, fostering an environment of continuous improvement and academic distinction.

Beyond his institutional contributions, Dr. Omar Al Ansari actively engages in national committees, lending his expertise to shape the future of education. He serves on committees such as the Advisory Committee for Higher Education, the Advisory Council for Qatar Leadership Academy, and the Advisory Council for the PhD program at the Doha Institute for Graduate Studies. His membership in various associations and unions related to education and engineering fields further attests to his dedication to professional development and the advancement of these disciplines.

As a distinguished representative of Qatar University, Dr. Al Ansari participates in numerous official and international forums. His presence in these gatherings ensures that Qatar University's vision, achievements, and expertise are recognized on a global scale.

Dr. Omar Al Ansari's journey and commitment to academic excellence have made him a leader in the field of higher education. His contributions continue to shape the landscape of Qatar University, empower students and faculty, and elevate the nation's educational standing.

Mr. Saleh bin Majid Al-Khulaifi

Mr. Saleh bin Majid Al-Khulaifi

Member

Saleh Majid Al-Khulaifi is the Deputy Undersecretary for Industrial Affairs and Business Development at the Ministry of Commerce and Industry in Qatar. He is actively involved in driving industrial development, fostering entrepreneurship, and attracting foreign direct investment to shape Qatar's economic landscape.

Drawing on his extensive experience, Mr. Al-Khulaifi has held key positions in both the public and private sectors. Notably, he served as the Executive Director of Business Intelligence and Localization at Qatar Development Bank (QDB), where he spearheaded strategic initiatives aimed at strengthening domestic industries and promoting entrepreneurship.

In addition to his role at QDB, Mr. Al-Khulaifi has made substantial contributions to several governmental boards, including the Investment Promotion Agency and Qatar Incubation Center. He also serves as the Chairman of the Qatari German Medical Devices company and is a Member of the Board of Directors for ELAN Media. Furthermore, Mr. Al-Khulaifi is an active participant of the World Economic Forum's Global Shaper program.

Academically, Mr. Al-Khulaifi holds a Master of Science degree in Technology Entrepreneurship (MSc) from the University College London. He also completed his undergraduate studies at Carnegie Mellon University, earning a bachelor's degree in Business Administration.

Mr. Saleh bin Hamad Al Sharqi

Mr. Saleh bin Hamad Al Sharqi

Member

Saleh bin Hamad bin Jaber Al Sharqi currently serves as the Director General of the Qatar Chamber of Commerce & Industry.

Mr. Al Sharqi’s journey in the Qatar Chamber began on February 1, 1999. Throughout his tenure, Al Sharqi held various senior managerial and executive positions until he was appointed as the General Manager.

Saleh is entrusted with various responsibilities that contribute to the Qatar Chamber’s success and the country's economic development. He formulates and implements effective strategies aligned with the economic environment and the country's development goals.

Mr. Al Sharqi evaluates the impact of trade relations between Qatari business communities and their counterparts worldwide, facilitating the growth of international business opportunities in Qatar. Moreover, he manages financial resources and human capital within the Qatar Chamber, ensuring their development and reporting requirements to the Board of Directors and the General Assembly.

As a representative of the private sector, Saleh fosters close relationships with government authorities and relevant stakeholders, working to incorporate private sector requirements in legislation and policy development. He also actively contributes to official conferences and events, advocating for the interests of the business community.

Additional Official Positions

In addition to his role as the General Manager of the Qatar Chamber, Saleh holds several other high-level positions. He is the Chairman of the “Made in Qatar” Exhibition Committee , Chairman of the Resources and Events Committee, Vice President of the Board of Directors of the GCC Commercial Arbitration Center, and the Deputy Chairman of the Board of Directors of the Commercial Arbitration Center for the GCC Arab Gulf Countries.

He also serves as a Member to the Board of Directors for several entities, including:

  • Al Dar for Exchange Works
  • Thye Arab-German Joint Chamber
  • Joint Arab-British Chamber,
Saleh Al Sharqi is also a Member of the Executive Leadership Committee of the Federation of GCC Chambers, and a Member of the Minimum Wage Committee.

Mr. Al Sharqi also spearheaded two editions of the "Made in China" expo, strengthening economic and trade relations between Qatar and China.

Through his strategic vision, dedication, and commitment to promoting economic growth, Saleh continues to shape the future of commerce and industry in Qatar. Academia Academically, Saleh holds a bachelor’s degree in business administration, which he obtained in 2012. In 2014, he earned a master’s degree in business administration.

Mr. Mohammed bin Abdullah Al Khalifa

Mr. Mohammed bin Abdullah Al Khalifa

Member

Mohammed Abdulla Al-Khalifa currently holds the position of Director of General Services at Al Jazeera Media Network and is a member of the Board of WISA.

Mr. Al-Khalifa earned a Bachelor's degree in Economy Management with a specialization in Accounting from the Faculty of Administration and Economy in Qatar in 1997.

Mr. Al-Khalifa began his career as an Accounts Auditor and Financial Controller at the Audit Bureau, where he served from August 1998 to September 2006.

Following this, Mr. Al-Khalifa took on the role of Chief Financial Officer of the Qatar Project for the Reconstruction of Southern Lebanon from September 2006 to August 2007. He then served as the Financial Controller of the Seventh Doha Cultural Festival from September 2007 to July 2008.

From July 2008 to August 2009, Mr. Al-Khalifa assumed the role of Manager of Administrative and Financial Affairs at the Institute of Administrative Development. Subsequently, he became the Director of Administrative and Financial Projects of the institute from September 2009 to February 2010.

From February 2010 to March 2011, Al-Khalifa held the position of Manager of Planning and Development at the Institute of Administrative Development. He then served as the Director of Human and Financial Resources at the institute from March 2011 to October 2013.

In August 2013, Mr. Al-Khalifa joined Al Jazeera Media Network as the Manager of Human Resources Services. Since July 2014, he has been serving as the Director of General Services at Al Jazeera Media Network.

Mr. Al-Khalifa's extensive experience and dedication have contributed significantly to the success and growth of the organizations he has been associated with. His expertise in financial management, administrative affairs, and human resources has been instrumental in ensuring the smooth operations of Al Jazeera Media Network.

His Excellency Dr. Ghanem bin Mubarak Al Ali

Member

Assistant Undersecretary For Culture Affairs:

Dr. Ghanim bin Mubarak Al-Ali is the Assistant Undersecretary for Cultural Affairs at the Ministry of Culture / Qatar and is also the Director General of the Qatar Center for Cultural and Heritage Events, and the General supervisor of National day celebrations committee.

Dr. Ghanem has extensive experience in several fields such as culture, investment, banking, Information technology and has accumulated great experience in policymaking and strategies development related to governance and sustainability.

He held several active positions during his career, including a cultural advisor at the Ministry of Culture, Director of Marketing And human recourses at Qatar Media Corporation, Investment Consultant at Aspire and Katara, H.H Representative Office in the Amiri Diwan, in addition to chairing several committees, as he served in several positions in the State Audit Bureau.

He obtained several certificates during his educational career, a doctorate in management and Higher Education, In addition to a master’s degree in accounting from the United Kingdom.and a bachelor’s degree in economics and accounting from Qatar University.
